About Me

I’m a UX designer with a background in landscape architecture. In my current and previous roles, I focused working on residential and commercial projects where I specialized in creating digital drawings and other project deliverables. In addition, I gained visual design skills through creating project presentations, renderings, and image boards. For these projects, I adapted the design based on the client’s goals, and ensured that their wants and needs were met. The opportunity to be more creative and focus on the user experience is what led me to transition into a career as a UX designer. I aim to create digital products that are simple, easy to use, and visually appealing.
